Check the Bias of a News Source

In an era of increasingly defined by the proliferation of misinformation and polarization, it's important for internet users to have context for what's on their screen. This microservice uses natural language processing to analyze patterns of bias on any news website in real time. Each time a url is submitted, dozens of the most recent articles are collected and analyzed for a variety of factors.

The process takes 5 to 15 seconds so please be patient!
